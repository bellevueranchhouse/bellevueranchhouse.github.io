Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I apply?
A: The first step to applying is to send an application located on the contact page.
Q. Where do I send the application?
A. Please send it to bellevueranchhouse@gmail.com
Q. Are pets allowed?
A. Sorry no pets are allowed the owner's family has a severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis) to animals.
Q. Is it co-ed?
A. Yes. There are no gender restrictions, the house tends to be co-ed.
Q. What is due when I sign?
A. First month's rent, last month's rent, and a security deposit. If your rent is $450, then first month's rent is $450, last month's rent is $450 and your security deposit is $450 totaling $1350. You will also need to send copies of your driver's licenses.
Q. Is the security deposit refundable?
A. It's fully refundable if you move out with the room in the same condition that you received it in. You must take photos and video upon move out.
Q. What is first month's rent?
A. First month's rent covers the first month you reside in the apartment, typically this is August 15th to September 14th.
Q. What is last month's rent?
A. Last month's rent covers the very last month you will reside in the apartment, typically this is July 15th to August 14th. https://legalbeagle.com/13724374-last-months-rent-how-does-it-work-in-california.html
Q. How do I view the house?
A. Please send an application located on the contact page and an email with your cell phone number and the owner will coordinate a viewing with an existing tenant.
Q. What are the steps after viewing and the Zillow application?
A. You and your co-signer will fill out a mysmartmove application to do a criminal background check and soft credit check.
Q. Do I need to include my social security for the Zillow application?
A. No
Q. Is internet included?
A. The students are responsible for purchasing a contract and splitting it among tenants which tends to be cheaper based on student promotions.
Q. Can I share the room?
A. No.
Q. Can my parent or friend view the room for me?
A. The owner strongly prefers to communicate with the future tenant, and for them to visit in person.
Q. Is there a video of the place?
A. Here's a video of the master, but is likely not available. https://imgur.com/a/E6bcFJp
