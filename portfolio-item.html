Sunset Walkway

Client: Sailor Bob Photography

Category: Art

Website: Website

We were free diving, and I knew that with only one lungful of air I wouldn't be able to get back to the surface if this turned out to be the wrong cave. These, and other negative thoughts spun through my mind as I breathed deeply and prepared to submerge.

My guide placed his snorkel in his mouth, nodded once, and dove beneath me with a powerful flick of his fins.
