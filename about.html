About the House

A comfortable and convenient home for UC Merced students.

Prime Location

  • ~2-3 minute walk to Cat Tracks bus stop with direct routes to campus.
  • Approximately 3.5 miles from UC Merced.
  • A quick ~6 minute drive to the university.
  • A pleasant ~15 minute bike ride to campus.

A Studious Environment

  • Respectful, clean, and quiet housemates preferred.
  • Shared common areas: kitchen, living room, and dining room.
  • Shared appliances include washer, dryer, fridge, and coffee machine.
  • Find individual room details on our pricing page.
